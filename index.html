My Services

From Development to Finance — Multi-Skilled Solutions

I bring a unique blend of technical and financial expertise. With over 4 years of experience in bookkeeping for real estate businesses, plus a strong background in WordPress development and custom web solutions, I help clients streamline operations and grow their online presence — all while keeping their finances clean and compliant.

Bookkeeping & Payroll

I handle daily bookkeeping, bank reconciliations, accounts payable and receivable, payroll preparation, and full tax document organization. With experience using QuickBooks, Xero, and bill.com, I ensure every record is accurate, every deadline is met, and your financial data stays audit-ready.

WordPress Development

As a WordPress developer, I build, customize, and maintain responsive websites. From Elementor layouts and custom CSS tweaks to real estate listing systems and CRM integrations — I deliver user-friendly sites that perform smoothly across devices.

Real Estate Bookkeeping

I specialize in managing complex real estate transactions, including escrow tracking, Schedule E receipt consolidation, and full property financial oversight. My role supports realtors and investors with precise records and on-time tax filings.

CRM & Systems Integration

Beyond coding, I streamline business operations with CRM data management, automated workflows, and custom integration between WordPress, sales tools, and accounting platforms — so your team can focus on growth, not paperwork.

I believe that strong systems, clear communication, and clean code are the foundation of any successful project. Whether you need your books balanced, your site modernized, or your workflows automated, I’m here to help you get it done right.
